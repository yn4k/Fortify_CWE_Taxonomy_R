							<h1>
							   Poor Style: Identifier Contains Dollar Symbol ($)
							</h1>
							<h2>ABSTRACT</h2>
							<p>
							  Using a dollar sign ($) as part of an identifier is not recommended.
							</p>
							<h2>EXPLANATION</h2>
							<p>
							  Section 3.8 of the Java Language Specification reserves the dollar sign ($) for identifiers that are used only in mechanically generated source code.<br><br><b>Example:</b><br><br></p>
<pre><br>int un$afe;<br></pre>
